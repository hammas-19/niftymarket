Connecting with Potential Clients on Their Level

We have found that marketing for lawyers has its own unique set of challenges compared to any other industry.

  1. Like it or not, a stigma exists and people naturally don't trust lawyers.
  2. Law is complicated. Most people don't understand the intricacies of law and are intimated at the thought of needing to contact an attorney.
  3. You all look the same. Meaning graduating Magna Cum Laude or having an Avvo 10.0 rating don't help you stand out. Everyone advertises that about themselves.

Video is the solution to these three challenges. Video allows you to connect with your clients on a deeper personal matter. It humanizes you and helps squash the trust concerns. It allows you to explain complicated law concepts in your own spoken words and it differentiates you from every other attorney out there. Telling your story through video is crucial today.
