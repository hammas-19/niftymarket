Who We Are

Nifty was started in 2009 with an objective to bring non-agricultural jobs to Burley. To do this we knew we would need to be creative and build a company culture that attracted the right kind of people. Because of this drive our core values became: We believe in being AWESOME. (Hover over the boxes to learn more about each attribute.)

At Nifty we believe there is a right way to do marketing for law firms. We are extremely passionate about helping law firms achieve success online. We live and breath marketing for law firms and travel the country talking about it. It is our goal to help lawyers get back in the “lawyer seat” and do what they are best at. To accomplish this we become a true partner and friend with the law firms that join the Nifty team. We become an integral member of the team and thrive on the success of the lawyers we work with.

We take pride in being insanely transparent and honest in everything we do. We are a bunch of passionate marketers in a small farming community with good values and even better work ethics.
