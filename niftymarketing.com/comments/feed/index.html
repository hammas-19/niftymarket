I’ve been trying to convince them to do local landing pages but they are against the idea for business reasons not SEO reasons. I was hoping this post would help me convince them.

Here’s the problem. The site you picked doesn’t show up in Google for “lawyers appleton wi”. The main domain shows up for “lawyers appleton wi social security” but not this specific page. The site also doesn’t show up for “social security attorney” unless you set the search location to Two Rivers, WI where their actual office is.

So I guess my point is that Google may not penalize you since the site is showing up for certain searches, but it’s also not rewarding you by putting you in the search results for locations you’re not in unless the query is very specific. I like the idea of adding the “Communities We Serve” section, but what if any is the benefit from an SEO perspective?
